## What it is

A crypto payment card within the Bitget Wallet ecosystem, enabling users to spend digital assets at merchants worldwide.

## Why it matters

Crypto payments are still a friction point for most users. This project aimed to bridge the gap between crypto holdings and everyday spending.

## What I did

Worked on product strategy, growth loops, and partnership integrations. Focused on onboarding flow optimization and conversion rate improvement.

## Outcome

Grew active card users from zero to a meaningful base. Learned a lot about regulatory navigation, payment infrastructure, and what actually drives adoption.
