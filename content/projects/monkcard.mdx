## What it is

A cross-chain payment card that lets users spend crypto at any merchant that accepts card payments.

## Why it matters

Most crypto cards are tethered to a single chain or ecosystem. MonkCard was built to work across chains from day one.

## What I did

Led the product design and go-to-market strategy. Worked with engineering on the card infrastructure, partnership integrations, and user experience.

## Outcome

Shipped to 50K+ users in the first quarter. Learned a lot about regulatory complexity in Southeast Asia.
