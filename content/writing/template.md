Start with a hook. First paragraph should draw the reader in — a contrarian take, a personal story, or a surprising observation.

## Section Heading

Write in short paragraphs. 2-4 sentences each. Your audience is scanning, not reading deeply.

Use bullet points when listing things:

- Point one
- Point two
- Point three

## Another Section

End with a takeaway. What should the reader remember or do differently after reading this?

Keep it simple. Ship it.
