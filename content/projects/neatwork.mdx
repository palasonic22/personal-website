## What it is

An AI-powered workflow tool that helps Singapore small businesses automate repetitive tasks — from invoice processing to customer communication.

## Why it matters

SMEs in Singapore run on WhatsApp and spreadsheets. There's a massive gap between what's possible with AI and what's actually being used.

## What I did

Built the MVP solo. Designed the user experience around WhatsApp-first workflows. Tested with 10 businesses before launch.

## Outcome

Still iterating. Early feedback is promising — businesses are seeing real time savings.
